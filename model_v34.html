What changed in v34
Tracking layer only — no live betting change. Every runner now gets a reliability tier (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH) computed from race type, field size, condition and distance, written to the database for reporting.

Backtested a live rule and rejected it. The natural next step — widen the market-rank gate to MR3-5 specifically in LOW-reliability races — was tested directly against history. It didn't hold up: the effect wasn't specific to low-reliability segments (widening helped a little in high-reliability races too), and the one promising sub-band (MR3-5, overlay 80-300%, LOW tier: +6.6% to +10.4% ROI) sat on only ~53 bets — the same thin-sample territory that produced false leads twice already this week.

New shadow category instead. That specific band (MR3-5, LOW tier, overlay 80-300%) is now tracked with $0 stake going forward, same discipline as MR2 Provincial. Revisit once it reaches 30-40 bets.
