Betting gates by race type
A horse must clear the v12 score bar for its race type, and trip none of the applicable gates below
Applies to every bet, every race type

Jockey floor

Strike rate must clear a minimum: 8% metro, 6% provincial, 5% country.

Distance block

Races 1601m or longer are blocked everywhere.

Dead zone

Priced close to fair value (0-30% overlay) — not enough margin to bet.

Overlay caps

Top favourite blocked above 200% overlay. Second favourite blocked above 100%.

Negative overlay

Priced worse than fair value is blocked, with a small allowance on wet tracks.

Second-favourite override

Can still qualify if it outscores every top favourite in the field.
Metro
Minimum score to qualify: 12 (10 for on-pace/backmarker styles). Higher bar for small/medium fields.
Jockey must be within 5% of the field's average strike rate.
Saturdays: overlay capped at 80%, tighter than the standard cap.
Saturdays: a high score (14+) is blocked unless on-pace — leaders, midfielders and backmarkers with big scores have underperformed.
Genuine front-runners with a positive overlay are blocked.
Provincial
Minimum score to qualify: 11 (10 for on-pace/backmarker styles).
Second favourites above 100% overlay aren't blocked outright — tracked as shadow bets while data is gathered.
Genuine front-runners with a positive overlay are blocked, same as metro.
Country
Minimum score to qualify: just 8 (7 for on-pace/backmarker styles) — the lowest bar of any race type.
Heavy-track racing only qualifies with overlay between 20% and 100% — outside that band it's blocked entirely (new in v33).
Front-runners are not blocked here — leading works well in country racing.
Hong Kong
Uses its own scoring engine, separate from the main model.
All Hong Kong bets are blocked on Saturdays — the market is extremely efficient on the biggest race days.
What changed in v33
Country heavy overlay cap replaces hard block. v32 blocked all country-heavy bets outright (-25.1% ROI, 39 bets). Root-cause analysis found the damage was concentrated in the 100%+ overlay tail (7 bets, 0 wins). The 20-100% overlay band on the same sample returned +51.7% ROI (9 bets, 5 wins). v33 now allows country-heavy bets through only within that band.

Sample size is small (9 bets) — this is being monitored closely and will revert to a hard block if it doesn't hold up over the next 20-30 country-heavy opportunities.
