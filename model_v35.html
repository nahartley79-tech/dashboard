S19 — Head-to-head rematch manual step
Beat today's rival(s) last start+1 per rival
Validated as real and common: 95% of races contain a rematch pair, 40% with a usable recorded outcome. Not yet automated — requires a DB query for today's field before scoring, passed in via rematch_data.
What was tested and rejected
Handicap Rating — was captured by an older pipeline (still present for 35% of historical runners), dropped since. Tested directly: win rate by rating rank was flat (9-12% across ranks 1-5), essentially no gradient. Likely redundant with weight, since the rating's entire purpose is to set the weight that then levels the field. Not added.
Data audit that led here
Cross-referenced all 128 CSV columns against actual usage in the scoring engine — roughly 100 were completely unused. Most were reasonably redundant (raw run counts underlying an SR already used, duplicate season windows). Barrier, Last Start Distance Change, and 2U%/3U% stood out as real, non-redundant gaps and are now built. Age and jockey/trainer "This Season" splits remain untested candidates for a future version.
